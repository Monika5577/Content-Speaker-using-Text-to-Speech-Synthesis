Text to Speech

Text to Speech service understands text and natural language to generate synthesized audio output complete with appropriate cadence and intonation.

The following demo enables you to enter your own text and we offer for speech-enabling websites, Dashboard giving a voice to online documents and or making your online/offline content more accessible with text to speech. it is available for web pages, tables and charts.

Features

  • Read the text content appropriate in more human like.
  • Read Highlight and select specific text .
  • Read Table with table head.
  • Narrate insights of Charts.

Benefits for Content Consumers

  • people who are working on charts need to only listening they will get insights of charts.
  • Spend less time for reading.
  • People with learning disabilities.
  • People who have literacy difficulties.
  • People who speak the language but do not read it.
  • People who multitask.
  • People with different learning styles.

Benefits for Content Owners and Publishers and company

  • Increase the accessibility of your online content for those with visual impairments or reading difficulties.
  • Reach a larger percentage of the online population, including those whose native language is different from the language of your website or mobile app. Present your online content in a way that increases comprehension.
  • Make it easier for people to access your online content on mobile devices. Provide seniors with a more comfortable way to access your online content.
  • Diminish the digital divide, increase citizen engagement, and strengthen your corporate social responsibility by ensuring that your information is available in both written and audio format.
